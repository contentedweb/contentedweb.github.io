CASP International Network

6 September, 2011

The CASP International Network (CASPin) is an international collaboration which supports the teaching and learning of critical appraisal skills, and in particular helps other people set up sustainable training programmes.  CASPin grew out of the work of the Critical Appraisal Skills Programme in Oxford.

---

CASPin is one of the earlier sites to use Minervation's in-house CMS, Phaedrus, as work started in it in early 2005. It is a fairly standard site which could probably do with a little attention.  This is actually unusual for sites created by Minervation, which are, on the whole, well maintained.

Launch date: April 2006
