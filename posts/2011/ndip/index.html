NDIP

2 October, 2011

Minervation launched the National Dental Inspection Programme (NDIP) website in September 2010. It is a Scottish website for registered dentists only who are given access to the site using a code provided by their NHS Trust.  The site is essentially an application to test the dentist's ability to identify dental problems using a Standardisation Test. Users can retake the test as often as they want. Once they pass, a personalised PDF certificate is available for them to download.

---

In September 2011 the site was update to include a second test, the Charting and AMR Test.

Launch date: September 2010
