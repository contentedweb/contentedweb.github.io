Echternach 2 Circular Walk

9 May, 2022

A nice 7.2km walk around Echternach lake. It started off with a steep climb into the forests on one side of the lake. The path returns to the level of the lake after about 4km and then winds around the lake, passed the Youth Hostel and back to the starting point. It was a lovely walk on a wonderfully warm and sunny day and the dog had a great time.
