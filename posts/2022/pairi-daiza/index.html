Pairi Daiza

6 June, 2022

On the last day of this long weekend round this neck of the woods, we decided to visit the zoo Pairi Daiza. It is considered to be one of the best zoos in Europe.

It's certainly worth the visit as it's a wonderful day out with lots of animals to see in lovely landscaped environment. Animals being animals and not toys, means they are generally just lying around. However, fascinating they still are. The panda's are amazing. You do wonder how they ever survived, only eating bamboo, meaning they have to eat most of the time. Then they sleep the rest of the time, on top of which the female is only fertile three days in the year. Still, nature and evolution finds a way!

I also bought some the beer made locally in the Cambron monastery. Tastes very nice indeed.
